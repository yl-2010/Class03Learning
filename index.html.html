Here are my three favorite things about this school (compared to my old school):

  1. Smaller Class Sizes
  2. Longer Class Times
  3. Study Halls

Smaller Class Sizes:

The biggest improvement I noticed between EPS and EMS (my old school) was the size of classes. At EMS, classes had between 25 and 30 people on average, with no cap on the number. However, EPS classes are normally closer to 15 people, with a cap at 18.

18>30

18 is definitely greater than 30.

Study Halls:

The final thing that I like about EPS are the study hall / free periods. They let me get my homework done in school, so I'm free at home. In Middle School, I had to spend a lot of time at home doing homeowork, so this is a big improvement.
